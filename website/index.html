THE PROBLEM

There is a general failure rate of 90% for clinical trials due to undesirable characteristics of the drug, most commonly being toxicity

THE FUTURE

- Accelerating clinical trial and drug discovery, eliminating toxicity within the byproduct.
- Empowering the scientific community around the world with a machine learning model to understand drug mechanisms to optimize research.

SMART uses machine learning to extract meaningful biological information for any compound, using only the chemical structure. This technology has the potential to expedite early screening in any drug discovery research.
